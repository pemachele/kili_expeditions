6 Days / 5 Nights

Arusha NP / Lake Manyara / Serengeti/ Ngorngoro/ Tarangire

Learn more
Day 1 - Early in the morning drive from Arusha or Moshi to Arusha National Park. You will participate in a half day walking safari accompanied with armed ranger and a half day game drive. Late in the evening drive to camp/lodge for dinner and overnight.

Day 2 - Leave the lodge/camp drive to Tarangire National Park for game viewing with picnic lunch. Dinner at TarangireSopa lodge or Kigongoni Campsite.

Day 3 - Early after breakfast drive to Serengeti National Park with picnic lunch. Dinner and overnight in the heart of Serengeti, Seronera Wildlife Lodge or Seronera Campsite

Day 4 - Morning Serengeti Game viewing with picnic lunch. Afternoon drive back to Ngorongoro.Dinner and overnight at Ngorongoro Wildlife lodge or Simba Campsite.

Day 5 - A full day in the Ngorongoro Crater. Lunch will take place at the picnic site at the crater floor. In the afternoon, drive to the top of the Great Rift Valley for overnight with a view to Lake Manyara or Twiga campsite
